Lasagna

Make yourself

ingredients

  • Two tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced Beef
  • olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta with olive oil and salt
  2. In a separate pan, stir fry minced beef
  3. Add cayenne, salt and cover cook on low heat
  4. Strain the pasta and add to the cooked minced beef mixture
